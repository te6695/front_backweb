The principles of design: Balance Balance in design is the distribution of elements of the design. Balance in web design is found in the layout. The position of elements on the page determines how balanced the page appears.

Balance can be achieved through the use of color, texture, shape, size, spacing, and other elements. When creating a balanced design,

it is important to consider the overall layout and how each element is placed in relation to others. For example, if you have a large image on one side of the page, you will need to add something of similar size on the other side to create balance.
